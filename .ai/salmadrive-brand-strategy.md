Section 01 — Logo Design

Logo Concept & Anatomy

A speed-forward monogram — the Salma Technology ST mark evolved into a dynamic SD identity, enclosed in a precision chrome ring with a signature metallic speed slash.

⭕

The Chrome Ring

A precise circular form rendered in metallic silver gradient — referencing wheel rims, precision engineering, and the circular motif already present in the Salma Technology parent logo. Projects reliability and premium positioning.

S

The S — Sky Blue

Inherits sky blue from the Salma Technology S letterform. Bold and forward-leaning — echoes a vehicle in profile. The horizontal tail deliberately extends beyond the ring, suggesting motion breaking beyond boundaries.

T

The T — Emerald Green

The T from the parent ST mark anchors the right side. In the sub-brand context it carries dual meaning: the crossbar reads as a road horizon line; the stem reads as a destination marker or landmark.

⚡

The Speed Slash

A horizontal metallic silver band cuts across the ring — the defining automotive signature. References speed lines, road markings, and chrome trim on a vehicle body. Unique and unmistakable.

◐

Heritage Arcs

Crimson (top-right) and Gold (bottom-left) arcs are inherited directly from Salma Technology — maintaining family brand recognition while silver and graphite add automotive premium depth.

Aa

Wordmark: Rajdhani

The wordmark uses Rajdhani — a geometric, condensed display font combining modern tech-forward character with automotive boldness. SALMA in chrome metallic gradient; DRIVE in sky-to-emerald gradient.

Section 03 — Brand Strategy

Strategic Foundation

Built to own the digital-first automotive retail space in Zimbabwe, leveraging Salma Technology's brand equity and ICT capabilities as a structural competitive moat.

🎯

Vision

To be Zimbabwe's most trusted virtual auto marketplace — where every Zimbabwean can buy, sell, or upgrade their vehicle and accessories confidently online.

🚀

Mission

To eliminate friction in the Zimbabwean automotive market by connecting buyers and sellers through a secure, transparent, technology-powered platform backed by Salma Technology's credibility.

🏆

Positioning

Premium-accessible.
 Not a cheap classifieds board — a curated, trust-verified marketplace. Premium enough for new cars; accessible enough for second-hand deals and small parts orders.

💎

Brand Promise

"Every listing is verified, every transaction is protected, and every deal is one click away." Trust + Convenience + Speed — the three things the Zimbabwean auto market currently lacks online.

🔑

Unique Value Prop

The only auto marketplace powered by a technology company — meaning the platform itself is the product. Built by tech people, for smart buyers and sellers.

🗣️

Brand Voice

Confident but not arrogant. Knowledgeable but accessible. Zimbabwean in heart — speaks directly to local market realities, pricing, and the trust gaps this brand solves.

Brand Pillars

01
Trust
Verified listings, seller IDs, deal protection — safety baked into every interaction.

02
Speed
Fast load, instant search, same-day enquiry responses. Speed as a feature, not an afterthought.

03
Choice
Cars, parts, accessories, dealers and private sellers — widest catalogue in market.

04
Pride
Zimbabwean-built, globally comparable. A platform the market can point to with pride.

Section 04 — Target Audience

Who We Serve

Four distinct customer segments, each with unique motivations Salma Drive is engineered to address.

The Digital Buyer
Ages 25–40 · Urban
Tech-savvy professionals in Harare and Bulawayo who research everything online. They want transparency, photos, specs, and price history — not a pushy showroom floor.

The Upgrader
Ages 30–55 · Mid-income
Existing car owners looking for accessories and genuine parts. High repeat-purchase potential. Loyalty-driven if they trust the platform's quality and consistency.

The Private Seller
All ages · Nationwide
Individuals selling or trading vehicles who need reach and credibility. Salma Drive gives their listing the prestige of a verified marketplace.

The Dealer / SME
Business · B2B
Small dealerships and parts shops seeking an affordable digital storefront. Tiered dealer packages, priority listings, and analytics drive B2B revenue.

Section 08 — Launch Roadmap

Phased Launch Plan

A bootstrapped, capital-efficient strategy that builds audience and trust before scaling inventory and paid marketing spend.

01
Months 1–2

Brand Launch & MVP Platform
Finalise all Salma Drive brand assets — logo files, colour tokens, domain, social handles
Launch salmadrive.co.zw with core vehicle listing, search, and enquiry functionality
Set up WhatsApp Business catalogue and Facebook Business page
Seed platform with 20–50 verified listings from known sellers and partner dealers

02
Months 3–4

Community & Content Growth
Launch "Salma Drive Verified" badge programme for trusted sellers
Weekly Instagram and Facebook content: featured cars, buying tips, market insights
Introduce accessories and parts category with at least 3 supplier partnerships
Implement free vs. premium listing tiers to begin monetisation

03
Months 5–8

Dealer Programme & Paid Features
Launch Dealer Dashboard: analytics, bulk uploads, and featured placement management
Onboard 5–10 Harare dealerships onto monthly subscription packages
Add vehicle comparison, saved searches, and buyer alert notification system
Begin paid Facebook and Instagram advertising campaigns

04
Months 9–12

Scale & National Presence
Expand coverage to Bulawayo, Mutare, and Gweru regional dealer networks
Launch Android-first mobile app (aligned to Zimbabwean market demographics)
Introduce escrow and deal protection payment feature
Explore automotive financing partner integrations for accessories BNPL options
